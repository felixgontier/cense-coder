Perceptual test

The perceptual test aims at assessing the impact of the analysis rate on intelligibility. Each subject is presented with short decoded utterances (average of 8 words). The test metrics are:

  • Average Intelligibility Score (AIS): Subjective score given by the participant. The score is given as an integer between 1 and 5 then mapped to the 0-1 range for statistics.
  • Intelligibility Ratio (IR): Ratio of correctly transcribed words.

Clean speech

An example of clean recorded speech used as the input of the coding scheme. The same set of sentences is studied for 6 different speakers: 3 male and 3 female. All recordings are made under the same studio conditions and have therefore the same quality.
